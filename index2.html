How to Make a Paper Airplane

Step 1: Get the Paper Ready

Start by folding the paper in half lengthwise.

Step 2: Fold the Wings

Next, fold the top corners down to the center. Make sure the edges are aligned perfectly.

Tips for Better Paper Planes

Use lightweight paper for longer flights
